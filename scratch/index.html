A Bit About Me 🙋‍♀️

I'm a student at BYU in Provo, Utah 🎓 — I've been here for about six months and I love it! Before BYU I served a full-time LDS mission in Brazil for a year and a half, which was one of the most life-changing experiences I could have asked for. I came back speaking fluent Portuguese and with a deep love for Brazilian culture 🇧🇷

When I'm not studying, you'll find me playing guitar or piano 🎸🎹, watching movies with my family at our favorite theater, doing family history research for my church, or rereading Harry Potter for the hundredth time 📚⚡

My room also has this awesome collage of photos from my life and old LIFE magazine issues. It's like a visual scrapbook of everything I love — music, travel, family, and memories.

Music 🎸🎹

🎸 Guitar

Guitar is my go-to when I need to decompress or express something I can't find words for. I love strumming along to songs I grew up with and learning new ones on my own.

🎹 Piano

Piano has a different energy — more deliberate, more emotional. Playing hymns on the piano is one of my favorite ways to feel grounded and at peace.

My Time in Brazil 🇧🇷

For 18 months, Brazil was home. I served as a full-time missionary across different regions, working with communities, teaching, and serving in whatever way I could.

I became fully fluent in Portuguese — something I'm incredibly proud of — and gained a deep appreciation for the warmth, music, food, and faith of the Brazilian people.

I now speak conversational Spanish too, which comes more easily thanks to the Portuguese foundation. Brazil gave me the world. 🌎

Movies with My Family 🎬

Some of my favorite memories are simple: piling onto the couch (or squeezing into theater seats) with my family to watch a movie. It doesn't matter what genre — a great story watched together hits different.

One of our go-to spots in Utah is the SCERA Center for the Arts in Orem — an outdoor theater with a classic, community feel that I love.
